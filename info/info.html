The Vision

Wellness Buddy was created to be your digital companion on your wellness journey. Combining retro cyberpunk aesthetics with modern technology, this project aims to provide practical tools that help you make healthier choices every day.

What's Available

Recipe Finder

The AI-powered Recipe Finder helps you create delicious, healthy meals with ingredients you already have in your kitchen. No more wasted food or last-minute grocery trips - just enter what you have, and Wellness Buddy will suggest recipes that match your available ingredients.

Wellness Guidance

Coming soon! Wellness Buddy will offer personalized advice on nutrition, exercise routines, meditation techniques, and more to support your overall wellbeing.

The Approach

At its core, Wellness Buddy is built on the belief that wellness should be:

  • Accessible - Health shouldn't be complicated or expensive
  • Personalized - Everyone's wellness journey is unique
  • Sustainable - Small, consistent changes lead to lasting results
  • Fun - Taking care of yourself should feel good, not like a chore

About The Developer

Wellness Buddy is a personal project born from a passion for both technology and wellness. I created this tool to combine my interest in retro gaming aesthetics with practical applications that can help people live healthier lives.

This project represents my belief that technology should not only look cool but also serve a meaningful purpose in our daily lives.
